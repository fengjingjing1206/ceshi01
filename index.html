Artistic Journey

Fengjingjing reimagines traditional Shan Shui painting in interactive form. Navigate through dynamically generated landscapes influenced by real Chinese geography and historical art patterns.

Game Features

  • Procedural landscape generation
  • 4 distinct artistic seasons
  • Dynamic weather system
  • Historical Chinese poetry integration
  • Relaxation mode with guided meditation
  • Cultural artifact collection system
